*Medilink – One Health One Card*

Overview

Medilink – One Health One Card is a healthcare management system designed to provide a centralized platform for patients and doctors. The application enables secure management of medical records, appointment scheduling, electronic prescriptions, and AI-powered disease prediction. The concept of a single digital health card allows users to access their healthcare information anytime and anywhere.

System Workflow
Patients and doctors register and log in to the system.
Patients can search for doctors and schedule appointments.
Doctors can view appointments and patient details.
Doctors generate digital prescriptions after consultation.
Brain MRI images can be uploaded for disease prediction.
CNN and SVM models analyze the MRI images and generate prediction results.
Medical records, prescriptions, and appointment details are stored in the database.
Patients can access their healthcare information whenever required.
